Story

Kodaline is an Irish rock band. Originally known as 21 Demands, the group includes Steve Garriga, Mark Prendergast, Vincent May and Jason Boland. Garrigan and Prendergast grew up in Swords, Dublin, and have known each other since childhood. Both attended high school in Colaiste Choilm. May joined the two in the group, followed by Boland in 2012. The group released three studio albums, In a Perfect World (2013), Coming Up Air (2015), with the third, Politics of Living, released in 2018.
